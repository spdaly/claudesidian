## Highlights
- The power of [generative AI](https://www.bcg.com/capabilities/artificial-intelligence/generative-ai) took the business world by surprise. It wasn’t until the release of ChatGPT that many executives truly appreciated the seismic impact of these large language models (LLMs), and many companies were left scrambling to keep up. As we enter what’s likely to be a period of permanent revolution, during which GenAI’s capabilities will progress much faster than businesses will be able to adapt, companies simply can’t afford to sit and wait. The next leap in [AI](https://www.bcg.com/capabilities/artificial-intelligence)—autonomous agents—could enter the mainstream in the next few years and promises to be even more transformative than today’s LLMs. [View Highlight](https://readwise.io/open/663383609) ^rw663383609
